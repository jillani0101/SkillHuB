Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SkillHub ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username, email address, and password (stored securely as a hash)
  • Skills and experience level you choose to add to your profile

Usage Information

  • Projects you create, join, or apply to
  • Comments and messages you post within the platform
  • Notifications related to your account activity

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with relevant projects based on your skills
  • To enable communication between you and your project teammates
  • To maintain platform safety, including reviewing reported content

4. How We Protect Your Information

Passwords are stored using secure one-way hashing and are never stored or visible in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your username, profile, and project activity are visible to other SkillHub users as part of normal platform functionality (for example, project owners can see who applies to their projects).

6. Your Rights

You may update your profile information at any time. To request deletion of your account or data, please contact us via our Contact page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of SkillHub after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.

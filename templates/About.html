About SkillHub

We built SkillHub to make it easier for students and builders to find the right people for the right projects.

Our Story

SkillHub started as a simple idea: finding teammates for student and side projects shouldn't depend on luck, group chats, or who you happen to know. We wanted a place where your skills speak for you, and where any project — big or small — can find the people it actually needs.

🎯

Our Mission

Connect skilled people with projects that need them, removing the friction of finding a team.

🤝

Our Values

Collaboration over competition. We believe the best projects are built by diverse teams working together.

🚀

Our Vision

A world where anyone with an idea — or a skill — can find their team in minutes, not weeks.

What We Offer

  • A searchable feed of active projects, filtered by required skills
  • A simple application and acceptance flow for joining teams
  • Built-in real-time chat for accepted teams
  • Profiles that showcase your skills and project history
